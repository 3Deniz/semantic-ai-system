# TITLE: Advanced Topology
# TYPE: knowledge
# SPACES: topology, semantic

## DATA

# Homotopi
homotopy continuous deformation between functions
fundamental_group π₁(X,x₀) group of loops up to homotopy
simply_connected space with trivial fundamental group
covering_space space that locally looks like product with discrete set

# Homoloji
simplicial_complex built from vertices, edges, triangles, tetrahedra
chain_group C_k formal sums of k-simplices
boundary_map ∂ maps k-simplices to (k-1)-simplices
homology_group H_k = ker(∂_k)/im(∂_{k+1})
betti_number rank of homology group

# Cohomoloji
cochain_group C^k dual to chain groups
coboundary_map δ dual to boundary map
cohomology_group H^k = ker(δ_k)/im(δ_{k-1})

# Kategori teorisi
category objects and morphisms between them
functor maps between categories
natural_transformation maps between functors
