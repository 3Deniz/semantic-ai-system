# TITLE: Topology and Group Theory
# TYPE: knowledge
# SPACES: abstract, semantic

## DATA

# Topoloji temelleri
open_set satisfies neighborhood of each point
closed_set contains all its limit points
continuous_function inverse image of open set is open
homeomorphism bijective continuous with continuous inverse
compact_set every open cover has finite subcover
connected_set cannot be partitioned into two disjoint open sets

# Grup teorisi
group satisfies closure, associativity, identity, inverse
abelian_group group where operation is commutative
subgroup is subset that forms a group
normal_subgroup gH = Hg for all g in G
quotient_group G/N where N is normal subgroup
homomorphism preserves group operation: f(a*b)=f(a)*f(b)
isomorphism bijective homomorphism

# Özel gruplar
cyclic_group generated by single element
symmetric_group S_n permutations of n elements
dihedral_group D_n symmetries of regular n-gon
matrix_group GL(n,R) invertible n×n matrices
